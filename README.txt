pfxpold - Postfix extensible policy daemon
------------------------------------------

The Postfix Extensible Policy Daemon aims to provide a flexible framework 
for policy delegation in the context of the Postfix MTA. It is written 
in Perl and accepts policy check routines in the form of Perl modules.

Please check the Wiki for more information: 
		
		http://pfxpold.wiki.sourceforge.net/

Thanks!
